WHY CHOOSE US?

At Distinct Landscape and Design, we're proud to have served the GTA and surrounding areas for over 25 years. Our commitment to quality work, exceptional customer service, and competitive pricing has always been our guiding principles. Whether you're dreaming of a backyard oasis for BBQs, a tranquil poolside retreat, or a playful space for your children, we ensure your home's exterior reflects your vision and lifestyle.
